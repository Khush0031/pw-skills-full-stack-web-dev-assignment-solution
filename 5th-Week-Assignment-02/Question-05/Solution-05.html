Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source fonts that you can use on your websites. It offers a wide selection of fonts, including the popular "Montserrat" font that is known for its clean and modern design.

You can easily integrate Google Fonts into your web projects by adding a link to the chosen font in your HTML document. With Google Fonts, you can enhance the typography of your websites and make them visually appealing.
